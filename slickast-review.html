What is Slickast.com?

This online store claims to sell many of the products listed above. Before you decide to shop at this online store, there are many things you need to know.

Slickast has been identified as one of the most fraudulent websites due to the following facts.

Different domain names and websites:

Its website name, “Happy Shop”, is different from its domain name. Legit websites don’t do such blunders. It has used the same domain name instead of its domain name in its policy pages. This is mostly due to copy-paste errors.

Contact Information:

Its parent companies are “Lushmo Pte. Ltd. and “COSMOR TUMBLR PE. LTD” can be found on numerous scam and troublesome sites like Serferi, Bruniss or Nestorest.

It has not provided the names of its parent companies as Lushmo Pte. Ltd. and COSMOR TUMBERLR PTE. LTD. but it could change its parent company’s name and address as many similar sites have done so.

Special Discounts and Sales Offers

Slickast lists many products for sale at steep discounts. Scam sites often offer such discounts in order to lure people to their scams.

Website security:

Slickast provided fake trust seal logos for logos such as McAfee and Norton. You can find the product details and checkout pages. If you shop on this site, your financial and personal information like your credit card number might be stolen.

Copied Content

There are many details on the Slickast website. This includes a theme match for multiple scam websites.

Deliveries and Customer Complaints

According to complaints from buyers of similar online stores, the delivery times, customer support and after-sales services are very poor.

Our Final Verdict:

These facts alone are sufficient to prove that Slickast online shop is fraudulent.
