Paul McCartney and His Wife Nancy Shevell Adopt a Lucky Dog Named Jet From the Labelle Foundation

Paul McCartney, a living legend for both being in the Beatles and for his tireless work advocating for animal rights, adopted a dog on July 24, 2023. Rover, who looks like a young Springer Spaniel or Spaniel mix, has been renamed “Jet” by McCartney and his wife Nancy Shevell. This follows the couple adopting a dog named Rose in early 2021.

Followers of @labelleforever were starstruck to see such an icon adopt a rescue. Many left lovely comments such as, “My favourite Beatle always loved animals and his newest ‘Jet’has a very beautiful face. Paul and Nancy are lovely people” and “Congratulations Jet! You are one lucky dog! You managed to score a home with a Beatle!!! A REAL BEATLE ! and of course his lovely wife! Now it’s time to work on Ringo! Enjoy your beautiful life!”

This lucky pup was rescued by The Labelle Foundation, a Los Angeles-based shelter that’s a favorite among celebrities. A few celebrities who adopted dogs from The Labelle Foundation include Dua Lipa, Kaia Jordan Gerber, Camila Mendes, Emma Roberts, Julie Bowen, Cara Delevingne, Glen Powell, and more.
