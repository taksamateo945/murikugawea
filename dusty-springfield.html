Who is Dusty Springfield:

Dusty Springfield is a famous Pop Singer. She was born on April 16, 1939 and her birthplace is London, England. Dusty is also well known as, British pop star who recorded “Son of a Preacher Man” in 1968, as well as other hits like “I Just Don’t Know What to Do with Myself.”

Dusty is originated from England. Her song “Son of a Preacher Man” was featured in the movie Pulp Fiction, which starred Uma Thurman.
