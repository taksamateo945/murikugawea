Lindsay Lohan on Mean Girls 2: They Should All Be Cheating Housewives

So fetch! Lindsay Lohan has some serious plans for Mean Girls 2.

The actress, 28, caught up with Time Out London this week in promotion of her new West End production Speed-the-Plow, and chatted about her beloved Mean Girls character Cady Heron and what she sees in her future.

“I was with Tina Fey the other day and I said we should do another Mean Girls,” Lohan told Time Out London (the two women reunited this past August and shared a photo together). “An older version where they’re all housewives and they’re all cheating. That would be really funny. I’ll harass Tina to write it.”

In the original 2004 classic, Lohan played the new girl in school who joins the popular Plastics clique, which is run by Regina George (Rachel McAdams) with Gretchen Wieners (Lacey Chabert) and Karen Smith (Amanda Seyfried). Written by Tina Fey, Mean Girls also starred Fey, Amy Poehler, Lizzy Caplan, and Ana Gasteyer, among others.
