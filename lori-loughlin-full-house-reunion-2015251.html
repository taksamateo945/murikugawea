Full House Cast Reunites For Creator Jeff Franklin's Birthday: Video

No Olsens? How rude! In the early hours of Sunday, Jan. 25, most of the Full House cast reunited at the birthday party of the beloved sitcom's creator Jeff Franklin. Lori Loughlin, who played Becky, posted a series of photos from the party with her former costar pals. 

"Good times w/ @JohnStamos Happy Birthday @fullhouseguy," Loughlin, 50, captioned a photo with her TV husband John Stamos. 

????

A photo posted by Lori Loughlin (@loriloughlin) on Jan 25, 2015 at 2:29am PST

The When Calls the Heart star also posed with Candace Cameron Bure and Jodie Sweetin who played sisters D.J. and Stephanie Tanner, writing, "It's looking like a Full House kinda night. @candacecbure @jodietweetin #fullhouse."

Bob Saget, who played Full House patriarch Danny Tanner, also attended the party, posing with Cameron Bure, 38, and Loughlin. 

The Dancing With the Stars finalist also shared a picture on Instagram getting cozy with her husband Valeri Bure, writing, "Caught- enjoying the evening with my husband. Date night. Photo credit @loriloughlin @melissacoulier."

Loughlin wasn't the only one documenting the event. Saget, 58, shared a picture with his on-screen brother-in-law on WhoSay, writing, "Great party last night apparently with my brutha @JohnStamos."

But Andrea Barber (Kimmy Gibbler) had the best Instagram of the night, sharing a video of the cast singing the sitcom's theme song.
