Live Nation Announces The Kidz Bop Live Tour

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Live Nation is bringing the family-friendly Kidz Bop brand as a live show to a concert venue near you this summer.

Kidz Bop, the leading kids music brand, will be front and center for the Kidz Bop Live 2024 North American tour.

For the tour, fans will be able to sing and dance along with the Kidz Bop Kids – Aleah, JJ, Shila, and Tyler – who will perform their fan-favorite hits such as “Dance The Night,” “greedy,” “vampire,” and more.

KIDZ BOP partnered with Red Light Management, the largest independent management company in the world, to represent the KIDZ BOP tour. Independent Artist Group (IAG) is also representing the KIDZ BOP tour. Additional fall tour dates will be announced later this year.

Additionally, a partnership with the Unicorn Academy will provide content on screen for fans to enjoy before the concert, in addition to other co-branded photo opps and activations on site. In ten select markets, lucky fans will be able to interact with a pop-up Unicorn Academy experience.
