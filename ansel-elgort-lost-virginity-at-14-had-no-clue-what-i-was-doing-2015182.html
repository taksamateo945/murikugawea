Ansel Elgort Lost Virginity at 14: &quot;Had No Clue What I Was Doing&quot;

Far from a movie scene! Fault in Our Stars hunk Ansel Elgort revealed in Elle's March 2015 issue that he lost his virginity at a young age, and the circumstances at the time were anything but romantic.

"I was 14," Elgort, now 20, told the mag. "I had no clue what I was doing, and neither did the girl… I didn’t even make the lighting good. That’d be one thing I’d do differently. It was, like, fluorescent, bright bedroom light, like, over the covers."

While the first time was a fumble, Elgort said he and the girl "definitely [did] not" wait to give it another go. "The only thing that made me feel better was doing it again," the Divergent actor explained, realizing that he may have come off too forward.

"Don’t make me sound like a playboy, because I’m not," he told the mag. "Cause if someone I’m monogamous with reads this, I don’t want them to be upset if it sounds like I’m a playboy."

That monogamous person happens to be his longtime love and high school girlfriend Violetta Komyshan. Us Weekly broke the news last month that Elgort and his sweetheart dancer were back together after breaking up last August.

"If you like someone and the sex is really good and you enjoy spending time together, why wouldn’t you make that person your girlfriend?" he mused to Elle. "Why go around dating random girls and having terrible sex when you can be with someone you really like?"

While Elgort did not confirm whether the person he lost his virginity to was Komyshan, the heartthrob alluded to her as he described his ideal significant other.
