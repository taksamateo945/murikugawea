Selena Gomez Weight Gain: A Tale of Self-Love and Empowerment

Selena Gomez Weight Gain: Selena Gomez is talking candidly about the physical effects of her lupus medicine. “It happens very regularly that I tend to hold a lot of water weight when taking it. I usually lose weight when I’m off of it,” Gomez posted on the video-sharing website. “I just wanted to say and encourage anyone out there who feels any sort of shame for exactly what they’re going through, and no one knows the real story.”
