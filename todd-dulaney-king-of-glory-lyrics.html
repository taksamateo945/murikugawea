Todd Dulaney King of Glory Lyrics

“King of Glory Lyrics” is a popular worship song by Todd Dulaney, written by Chris Tomlin, Matt Redman, and Jonas Myrin. It has been performed by various artists and is often sung in churches and worship gatherings. The lyrics of the song exalt God as the King of Glory and declare His majesty and power.

Todd Dulaney

Todd Dulaney is a gospel music artist and worship leader. He was born on December 20, 1983, in Maywood, Illinois, United States. Before pursuing a career in music, Dulaney had a successful career as a professional baseball player, having been drafted by the New York Mets and playing for various minor league teams.

However, in 2011, Todd Dulaney made a shift to pursue his passion for music and ministry. He released his debut album, titled “Pulling Me Through,” in 2013, which gained significant attention in the gospel music industry. Since then, he has released several albums, including “A Worshipper’s Heart” (2016), “Your Great Name” (2018), and “To Africa with Love” (2019).
