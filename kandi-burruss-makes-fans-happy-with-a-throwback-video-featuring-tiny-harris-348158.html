Kandi Burruss Makes Fans Happy With A Throwback Video Featuring Tiny Harris

Kandi Burruss shared a music video on her social media account from back in the day. She was singing with Tiny Harris, and fans are gushing over the two ladies in the comments.

‘TBT: My song “How Could You (Feel my Pain) from the album Kandi Koated! If you remember this album drop your favorite song below…’ Kandi captioned her post.

Kandi’s fans and followers were happy to see the tune posted on her social media, and they also told her about their favorite tracks from her.
