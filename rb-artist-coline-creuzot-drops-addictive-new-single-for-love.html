R&amp;B Artist Coline Creuzot Drops Addictive New Single &quot;For Love&quot;

Houston, Texas-born soulful R&B talent Coline Creuzot radiates warmth and romance in her new single “For Love”. With a sultry, unapologetic tone she sings of a vast love unlike any other. Her mesmerizing, velvety vocals float over a vibrant old school bounce, filled with epic horns and drums. She describes of the song, ”I love New Orleans Bounce music, so I went in and put my spin on it. The message is to embrace love (the good love), never settle. We all deserve to be loved in the way we desire.”

Creuzot is recognized for her rich, seductive air combined with masterful lyricism. Ever since she was young, she has been instantly drawn to music. That intense hunger is evident in this fiery offering. Each of her songs is a passionate ode to old-school R&B and she does the genre proud. Listening to her tracks will really change you so it is only fitting she create a song about a life-altering romance.
