Millie Bobby Brown and Noah Schnapp Make a Marriage Pact Celeb Secrets

Will Byers and Eleven live together in the latest season of Stranger Things, and soon, Millie Bobby Brown and Noah Schnapp may be doing the same.

In an interview with MTV News, the two actors shared that they made a pact to get married! Brown revealed, “We said if we’re not married by 40, we’ll get married together. Because we would be good roomies.” Brown did make it clear that this pact would be just as friends.

Brown did share the two wouldn’t be having kids. She said, “No kids, though, cause I could not deal with a child of yours.” This was a dealbreaker to Schnapp, but Brown went on to say that instead they’d have dogs together and sleep in “separate bedrooms for sure”.

This pact may have to hold off because Brown is currently in a relationship with Jake Bongiovi, who happens to be Jon Bon Jovi’s son. The two officially confirmed their relationship in June of last year, when Bongiovi posted a photo of the two with the caption, “bff <3”.

Fans will have to wait and see in 20 or so years whether the two costars have found other partners or if this pact will actually pan out!
