Top 8 Best Family-Friendly Seasons on Netflix

Once you are all sorted with the right internet connection, you can then have a smooth streaming session with your family.

1. Sweet Magnolias

This is the perfect and sweetest watch for your family.

Sweet Magnolias is a show all about a beautiful friendship between three women, who have been together since high school and live in a small town. They stood by each other through thick and thin, like sisters. It talks about the struggles of their married life, with kids. No matter what life throws at them, they have each other’s backs to fall on.

2. Shadow and Bone

Is your family into fictional series? Then Shadow and Bone can be the right fix for a family’s Netflix and chill time.

The series revolves around Alina, a girl who has supreme powers she did not know about. She can be the savior, but she falls into the hands of the enemy. Find out if she would be able to save her people or not.

3. The Good Place

The Good Place is a fun watch with the family. The plotline revolves around a bunch of people who died and ended up in “The Good Place” (Heaven), instead of “The Bad Place” (Hell). Even though they are fully aware, they do not deserve to be in a good place, it gets interesting to watch them turn themselves into better people.

4. Haunting of Hill House

How about you watch something a little on the scary-thriller side? If you are into this, ten Haunting of Hill House is the way to go. The show is about a close-knitted family that moves into a new place and begins turning it into their dream home. Little did they know, it would only be a part of their nightmares for the rest of their lives.

5. The Babysitter’s Club

Based on a book, this show is another perfect and light pick for your family binge session. Its plotline revolves around a babysitter’s club, that is formed by a group of friends. They always have their services available for the community. The Babysitters are only trying to make the people’s life slightly better.

6. Lost In Space

Getting lost anywhere in the world isn’t pleasing, so one can only imagine the dread that would follow if you were to get lost in an unknown place in SPACE!

Lost in Space takes you on a journey just like that. The journey of a family, that even though prepared to live on a different planet in space, was not ready for a crash landing on a whole other place entirely. It gets crazy, especially when the family is involved.

7. One Day At A Time

Life isn’t so easy, and One Day At A Time is all about the struggles a single Latina mom and her kids face in their lives every day. From love interests to finances, to racial differences; the world isn’t kind to anyone.

This is a fun watch with your family, all while it raises conversations about the issues people are facing in the contemporary world.

8. Friday Night Lights

The show revolves around a small town people where Friday Nights are the most important of all due to the football match happening weekly. The residents forget about their worries, and stress and come together to watch the town’s pride.

Friday Night Lights has got it all for you. Want something about football? Check. Would like a dash of drama with social issues? Check. Want to see some high school shenanigans? Check.

Wrapping Up

There you have it! The best series to watch with your family, according to us. Now all that’s left to worry about is picking the right snacks for the binge sesh.
